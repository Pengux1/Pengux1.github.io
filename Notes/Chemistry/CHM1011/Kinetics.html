Learning Objectives

  • Part One
    • Explain the concept of reaction rate.
    • Describe the conditions that affect reaction rates.
    • Write rate expressions.
    • Define the average rate and instantaneous rate and calculate these from data.
    • Know the form of a rate law.
    • Recognise the characteristics and write the rate law for zero, first and second order reactions.
    • Derive rate laws and reaction orders from experimental information.
    • Determine the differential form of a rate equation.
    • Recognise and manipulate the integrated rate laws.
    • Describe the kinetics of radioactive decay, using a rearrangement of the first order integrated rate law.
  • Part Two
    • Investigate how catalysts affect reaction rate.
    • Understand collision theory and it's role in reaction rates.
    • Investigate how temperature and activation energy effect reaction rates via the Arrhenius Equation.
