Classify ligands using the concept of denticity.


Ligands can be classified by the number of donor atoms that attach to a metal centre. This classification is known as the concept of ‘denticity’. Ligands with one donor atom are called monodentate; ligands with two donor atoms are call bidentate; three = tridentate, etc.
