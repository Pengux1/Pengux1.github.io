Introduction to Organic Chemistry

Learning Objectives

  • Distinguish the 4 classes of hydrocarbon.
  • Identify common functional groups.
  • Understand how structure impacts properties of simple alkanes.
  • Draw alkanes, alkenes and alkynes representing their 3D structure.
  • Distinguish between conformations of alkanes and alkene stereoisomerism.
  • Draw Newman projections, Chairs/Boats.
  • Assign stereochemistry of alkenes.
  • Write mechanisms for reactions of alkenes.
