Introduction to Transition Metals

Learning Objectives

  • Recall transition metals and identify their uses.
  • Review the periodic trends in ionisation energy, electron affinity, atomic radius including the transition metals.
  • Define an atomic orbital & discuss the different shapes and features, and how orbitals relate to quantum numbers with the emphasis on the d orbital.
  • Apply the aufbau principle, Hund's rule and the Pauli exclusion principle to write full and condensed electronic configurations for the transition metals in their neutral and ionic forms.
  • Discuss the use of the four quantum numbers.
  • Determine the various oxidation states that transition metals can adopt.
