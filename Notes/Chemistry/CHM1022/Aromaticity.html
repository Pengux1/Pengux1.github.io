Aromaticity


Learning Objectives

  • Draw benzene and understand its structure and unique stability.
  • Identify aromatic compounds and heterocycles and apply Hückel’s rule.
  • Name benzene molecules.
  • Identify the symmetry of substituted benzenes using Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (13C NMR).
  • Identify the structure of phenols and aniline.
  • Draw the resonance structures of phenol.
  • Connect reactivity and pKa of phenols and aniline to resonance.
  • Draw the mechanism for bromination.
