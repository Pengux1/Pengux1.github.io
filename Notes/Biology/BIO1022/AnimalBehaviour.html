Introduction to Animal Behaviour

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the approaches used to study behaviour.
  • Describe examples of innate and learned behaviours and attempt to understand when one should be favoured over the other.
  • Explain the link between mating systems and parental care.
  • Explain how sexual selection has favoured the evolution of mate choice.
  • Understand how inclusive fitness theory helps to explain altruism.
