Digestion

Learning Objectives

  • Describe the key stages of food intake, digestion & absorption.
  • Describe the function of the major regions of the mammalian digestive system.
  • Describe homeostatic control of food intake & energy management in the body.
  • Describe the basic principles and enzymes involved in carbohydrate, protein, and fat digestion that are described in the text.
  • Describe the regulation of digestive enzyme secretion in the gut.
  • Describe the basic adaptations for feeding in carnivores, herbivores and filter feeders.
