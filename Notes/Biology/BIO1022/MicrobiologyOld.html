Learning Objectives

  • Define the different types of microbes and describe the origins of the 3 domains of life.
  • Compare and contrast prokaryotic and eukaryotic cells.
  • Compare and contrast bacteria and archaea.
  • Describe how bacteria are observed using microscopy.
  • Describe the gram positive and negative cell wall.
  • Relate the steps in the gram stain procedure to the bacterial cell wall structure.
  • Describe the interactions of bacteria and their host.
  • Define the terms microbiota, pathogen, colonisation, infection and disease.
